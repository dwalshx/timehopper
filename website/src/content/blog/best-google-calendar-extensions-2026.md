# Best Google Calendar Extensions (2026): Quick View, Alerts, and Time Zones

**Direct answer:** The best Google Calendar extensions fall into three buckets: **quick-view (see your schedule fast)**, **alerts (don’t miss meetings)**, and **time zones (stop conversion mistakes)**. The right choice depends on your workflow—use this guide to pick in minutes.

---

## 1) Quick-view extensions
**Direct answer:** Best if you hate tab switching.

Look for: toolbar agenda, fast load, readable “next meeting.”

## 2) Alert extensions
**Direct answer:** Best if you’ve missed meetings due to notification failures.

Look for: reliable desktop alerts, snooze/dismiss, low noise.

## 3) Time zone extensions
**Direct answer:** Best for remote teams and travel.

Look for: “what time is it in X?”, multiple time zones, DST awareness.

---

## Trust signals (non-negotiable)
Avoid: surprise tabs, injected ads, excessive permissions.
