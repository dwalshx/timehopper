# Google Calendar Reminders Not Working? Fixes + a Redundancy Plan

**Direct answer:** When Google Calendar reminders don’t work, it’s usually a notification permission issue (browser/OS), Focus/Do Not Disturb, or reminder settings on the event itself. Run the checklist, then add redundancy for critical meetings.

---

## Fix checklist (do in order)

### 1) Confirm the event actually has a reminder
**Direct answer:** Some events have no notification configured.

Open the event → ensure there’s a reminder (e.g., 10 minutes before).

### 2) Check Chrome/site notification permission
**Direct answer:** If notifications are blocked, reminders won’t show.

Chrome Settings → Site settings → Notifications → allow, and ensure calendar.google.com isn’t blocked.

### 3) Check OS Focus / Do Not Disturb
**Direct answer:** Focus modes frequently silence reminders.

Allow Chrome (or disable Focus during work hours).

### 4) Check which device is receiving reminders
**Direct answer:** You may be getting them on another device.

Check phone, smartwatch, secondary laptop.

---

## Redundancy plan (so you stop relying on one channel)
**Direct answer:** Use multiple layers for important meetings.

A simple plan:
- Calendar reminder
- Phone reminder
- A lightweight meeting alert extension as backup

---

## FAQ

### Why do reminders work on my phone but not my desktop?
Usually browser permissions or OS notifications on desktop.

### Why do reminders arrive late?
Time zone mismatch or OS notification batching can delay alerts.
