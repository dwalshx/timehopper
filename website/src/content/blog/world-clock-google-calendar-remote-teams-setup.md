# World Clock + Google Calendar: The Fastest Setup for Remote Teams

**Direct answer:** The simplest remote-team time setup is: **(1) add a [secondary time zone](https://support.google.com/calendar/answer/37064) in Google Calendar**, **(2) keep a world clock visible for key cities**, and **(3) use a toolbar calendar peek** so you can check meetings without switching tabs.

---

## Step-by-step setup

### Step 1: Add a secondary time zone
Google Calendar Settings → General → Time zone → enable Secondary time zone.

### Step 2: Choose 2–4 “team cities”
Example: SF, NY, London, Bangalore.

### Step 3: Keep a world clock visible
If it’s hidden in a tab, nobody uses it.

## DST: the danger zone
Double-check key meetings during DST transition weeks.
