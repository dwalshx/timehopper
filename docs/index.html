🔒 Privacy Focused

We believe your schedule is yours. TimeHopper fetches calendar data directly from Google's API and stores it locally in your browser. No external servers attached.

Transparency & Data Usage

TimeHopper requests access to your Google Calendar (calendar.readonly) solely to display your events within the extension and provide local reminders. We also request access to your email (userinfo.email) to identify your calendar account.

We do not sell your data, viewing history, or schedule details. All processing happens locally on your device.
