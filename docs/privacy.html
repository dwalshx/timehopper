Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

TimeHopper ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome Extension designed to help you manage time zones and schedule meetings. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

TimeHopper accesses the minimum amount of data necessary to function:

  • Calendar Data: We access your Google Calendar events strictly to display them to you in the extension popup and to provide meeting notifications. This data is fetched directly from Google's APIs and is stored locally in your browser to improve performance. We do not transmit this data to any external servers or third parties.
  • User Preferences: using Chrome Storage, we save your settings (such as selected time zones, working hours, and notification preferences).
  • Analytics: We use aggregated, anonymous analytics (via Amplitude) to understand feature usage and improve the product. This data does not contain personally identifiable information (PII) or calendar details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your upcoming schedule and time blocks.
  • To provide desktop notifications for upcoming meetings.
  • To calculate time zone overlaps for team coordination.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.
