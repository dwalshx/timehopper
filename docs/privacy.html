Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

TimeHopper ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome Extension designed to help you manage time zones and schedule meetings. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

TimeHopper accesses the minimum amount of data necessary to function:

  • Calendar Data: We access your Google Calendar events strictly to display them to you in the extension popup and to provide meeting notifications. This data is fetched directly from Google's APIs and is stored locally in your browser. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.
  • User Preferences: using Chrome Storage, we save your settings (such as selected time zones, working hours, and notification preferences).
  • Analytics: We use aggregated, anonymous analytics (via Amplitude) to understand feature usage and improve the product. This data does not contain personally identifiable information (PII) or calendar details. No calendar content is ever sent to analytics.

Limited Use Disclosure

TimeHopper's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

No AI training

We believe your data belongs to you. We do not use any information from your Google Calendar to train Large Language Models (LLMs) or any other artificial intelligence systems.

Data Protection Disclosures

To provide maximum transparency for our users and for Google OAuth review, we explicitly disclose the following:

  • Limited Use: TimeHopper's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
  • Local Storage: All Google Calendar event data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (via Chrome Storage API). This data never touches our servers.
  • No Third-Party Sharing of Calendar Data: We do not share, sell, or transmit your Google Calendar event content to any third parties, including our analytics provider (Amplitude).
  • Minimum Permissions: We only request calendar.readonly to display your events and userinfo.email to identify your connected account.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your upcoming schedule and time blocks.
  • To provide desktop notifications for upcoming meetings.
  • To calculate time zone overlaps for team coordination.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. As noted above, we use Amplitude for anonymous product analytics, and Google APIs for fetching your calendar data directly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@timehopper.app.
